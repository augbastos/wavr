Wavr / home presence
Fused multi-modal sensing

Your home, understood. Without giving it away.

Wavr fuses network scan, WiFi CSI, camera pose and mmWave radar into one honest, explainable presence read per room, running entirely on your own hardware.

Private

Loopback-only by design. Zero cloud egress, no accounts, no telemetry. Off localhost, the app refuses to serve real data and self-switches to a simulator.

loopback · zero egress

Explainable

Every room shows why it reads occupied: which modalities agree, how strong the signal is. Confidence is agreement × strength, never a black box.

agreement × strength

Local

Runs on your own hardware: a spare mini-PC or a Raspberry Pi. No subscription, no vendor lock-in, no monthly bill. Your walls, your compute, your data.

your hardware · no subscription
