    Deep network diagnosis

    diagnosing…

    Runs the full set of network/source/mDNS/inventory checks at once and reports what's healthy, what got auto-fixed (safe, local-only — never your router or other devices), and what still needs you. Also checks 3 public DNS resolvers, but only while egress is allowed on this hub — blocked egress means this stays a gateway/LAN-only diagnosis.

    Network sensing

    local sensing — nothing leaves home

    These read standard network-discovery traffic already on your LAN (mDNS, UPnP, DHCP, etc.) to identify your devices better. Unlike the list above, these are not egress paths — they scan only your own network and nothing leaves home. Each collector is set from the hub's configuration — flipping one individually needs a hub restart, but the master switch above can pause all of them at once, right now.

    Privacy

    Privacy & security

    Wavr's contract with your home

    Your data

    Exports, right here on this device, what this panel has already loaded: presence history, house floor plan, and zones. Deleting data on the hub (“right to be forgotten”) needs a backend route — not in this version yet.

    Nothing leaves home by default. Four commitments hold for every Wavr installation:

    • Frames stay in memory only. Cameras process every frame in RAM and discard it — they never write to disk, never send video.
    • Vitals and positions exist live only. Breathing, heart rate, and movement paths exist in the live stream and are not retained beyond your hub.
    • All egress is opt-in. In the basic flow, only the narrator (History tab) sends data to the cloud — with confirmation, every time. Notifications (ntfy), MQTT, Home Assistant, MCP control, and a cloud Assistant engine are also optional egress paths from the hub: they ship off and only leave home if you turn them on.
    • Cameras start off. Every new camera (and every hub restart) starts off — turning it on is always your action.
